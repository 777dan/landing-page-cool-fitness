Yoga

Description:

Discover the benefits of yoga through a free class that focuses on the integration of breath and movement, promoting strength, flexibility, and mindfulness.

Benefits:

Functional Training

Description:

In a dynamic and functional workout that incorporates exercises to enhance everyday movement patterns, core stability, balance, and coordination.
